VIEW ON OPENSEA

10.000 pixelated ants

ANTs is a colony of 10.000 pixelated ants living on the blockchain. Some are small, some are big, some are cool, some are ugly, some prefer to work alone, some work together, some stay underground, some take risks by adventuring the open world, some even have laser-eyes, can you believe it? Every single ant is unique and brings value to the colony. During the emergence (mint), our ERC721A contract will prevent anyone from paying high gas-fees. In our colony the rules are very simple, ‘’the better you work, the better you eat’’, we are representing it throughout a C2E (Call To Earn) system. Of course, we will also learn all together how to become better. It will happen inside the Anthill (discord), still in construction, and will be ready once the ants are released.
